# Papa’s Boss Picks — OMNI HT/FT Gatekeeper v2.5.2

Public URL: `https://betspapa.com/boss-picks.html`

OMNI evaluates 44 active markets, but only six core markets may become a final Boss Pick:

1. First Half Over 0.5 Goals
2. Second Half Over 0.5 Goals
3. Home Team to Lead at Any Time
4. Away Team to Lead at Any Time
5. Home Team to Win Either Half
6. Away Team to Win Either Half

Full-match Over 0.5 is removed. Team and half 0.5 markets remain.

Prime starts at 87/100 rule points. Qualified starts at 80/100. The score is not a calibrated probability.

There is no maximum Boss Pick limit. Every fixture producing an eligible core selection is displayed.

BetsPapa uses available-data mode. Missing xG or event-order evidence blocks only the markets that require it. No missing data is invented.

Boss Picks remain free and public.
