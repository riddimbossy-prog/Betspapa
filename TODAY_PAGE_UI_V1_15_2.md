# BetsPapa Today Page v1.15.2

## Improvements

- Adds a Today at a Glance summary for All, Live, Pending, Settling and Settled fixtures.
- Adds direct match-state filtering from the summary cards and board tabs.
- Keeps one featured Papa analysis above the board.
- Uses a full-width, three-column fixture board on desktop.
- Uses two fixture columns on the Samsung Z Fold 6 unfolded display and compact tablets.
- Uses one readable fixture column on ordinary phones and the Z Fold 6 cover display.
- Moves the full filter set into a bottom sheet on mobile.
- Keeps date and match-state controls immediately accessible on mobile.
- Prevents team names, scores and market labels from pushing cards outside the viewport.
- Preserves live refresh, automatic settlement and the full explanation popup.

## Responsive targets

- Desktop: 3 fixture columns
- Z Fold 6 unfolded / 600–900px: 2 fixture columns
- Standard mobile: 1 fixture column
- Z Fold 6 cover / <=380px: compact 1-column layout
