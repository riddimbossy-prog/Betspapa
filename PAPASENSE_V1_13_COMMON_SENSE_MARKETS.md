# PapaSense v1.13 — Common-Sense Market Intelligence

Papa translates HT/FT stories into practical markets:

- 1/1 → home team to win either half.
- 2/2 → away team to win either half.
- 1/2 or 2/1 → GG when both teams score and both defences are leaky; otherwise Over 1.5.
- X/X, X/1, X/2, 1/X and 2/X → Draw in Either Half.
- Straight wins require the selected team's comeback ability and the opponent's lead-surrender history.
- Two teams that regularly attack for half-time leads can qualify First Half Over 1.5.
- Actual Team Over 0.5 odds below 1.20 are rejected as poor value; Team Over 1.5 is used only when the 2+ route also qualifies. Odds are never invented.

Added markets: Win Either Half, Draw Either Half, First Half O0.5/O1.5, Second Half O0.5, and side-specific Team O1.5.
