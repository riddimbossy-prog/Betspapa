PAPASENSE ENGINE

How the prediction engine thinks

  1. Resolve the true home and away teams before translating W/W into 1/1 or 2/2.
  2. Compare opposite HT/FT transitions from both teams and smooth small samples.
  3. Build full-time, half-time, GG, Over 1.5, Over 2.5 and Under 3.5 signals.
  4. Apply latest scoring, conceding, comeback, volatility and one-sided dominance thresholds.
  5. Reject unstable or contradictory markets and publish one strongest qualified pick.
