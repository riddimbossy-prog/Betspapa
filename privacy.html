BETSPAPA Privacy

Privacy Policy

Information used by the site

BetsPapa uses the information required to operate football analytics, protect the service and understand technical performance. Private administrator credentials are never stored in public website files.

Third-party services

Fixture, result and team-history information may be processed through connected data providers, Render and Supabase. Their availability and policies also apply.

Future accounts and payments

Before public accounts, subscriptions or payments are enabled, BetsPapa should publish the exact information collected, the reason for collection, retention periods and deletion options.
