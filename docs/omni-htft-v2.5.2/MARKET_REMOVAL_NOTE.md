# Market Removal Note — v2.5.2

`MATCH_OVER_0_5` (full-match total Over 0.5 Goals) has been removed because its typical price is too low to provide worthwhile value.

This removal is narrow. It does **not** remove:

- Home Team Over/Under 0.5
- Away Team Over/Under 0.5
- First Half Over 0.5
- Second Half Over 0.5

The engine now evaluates 44 markets and can select from six core markets.
