WHY DOUBLE CHANCE WAS OVER-SELECTED
-----------------------------------
The previous version compared raw probabilities. Double Chance combines two
outcomes, so its raw number was naturally larger than a straight win, GG or
goal line. v1.6 compares each market against its own threshold and applies a
protection penalty. Double Chance now wins only when it is genuinely the best
common-sense route.
