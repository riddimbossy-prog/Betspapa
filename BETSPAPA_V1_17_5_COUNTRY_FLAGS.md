# BetsPapa v1.17.5 — League Country Flags

Country flags are derived in the browser from the existing `league.country` field.
No Supabase column or SQL migration is needed.

## Coverage

- Today board and result table
- Papa’s Pick, Aggressive, Safer and Venue Pattern
- Consensus Bankers and OMNI Boss Picks
- Results Intelligence
- Live & Fixtures
- League filters and explanation dialogs

International competitions use regional symbols such as 🌍, 🌎, 🌏 and 🇪🇺.
England, Scotland and Wales use their football nation flags where supported by the device.
